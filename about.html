My Story

First of all, thank you for clicking in and being interested.

I am a Designer with coffee addiction and experience in development. I found my passion for design through the program called Interactive Media Design where I learned about creating interactive experiences( App, Web, Game, In-person, Motion). After a few projects, I fell absolutely in love with it and have been living and breathing design ever since

But things were not that easy. I had a degree in Mechanical Engineering and worked as a Manufacturing Engineer for some time but I had no passion for the position. So Eventually I decided to follow my heart and pursue a career that always interests me. But the Design Thinking for creating manufacturing process and the people management skill are what I took with me to the next chapter

Outside of design and development. I am also a Robotics/Programming Instructor for high school students where I lead them to Vex Robotics Competition / Hackathon / Game Jams

Also a huge Basketball Fan. Skipped a lot of classes back in my engineering days just to go hoop

So hit me up if you're interested for a converzation. I'm always down for some coffee. You can find my contact information below in the footer
