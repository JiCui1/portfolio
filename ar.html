Step 1. Quantitative Research (Survey)

Participants: 75
Questions: How likely are you going to use AR in your work

Result: Small Business Owners/Marketers, Educators and Artists have expressed strong interests in using AR in their work

Step 2. Primary Research (User Interview)

Interview Questions
  • 1. How do you see yourself using AR?
     (Discover potential usage of AR)
  • 2. How do you get assets for AR?
     (Evaluate user frictions in getting assets)
  • 3. Is there a specific problem you can solve with AR?
     ((To see if there's an immediate need)
  • 4. How much do you think AR can help you with your work?
     (To see how valuable our service is to the user)

Synthesizing Research

From the user interview that is conducted above, it seems clear that the best matching target user group for our product is Artist for the reason that there is a specific problem it can solve

Target User Problem Statement

Artists often struggle to have exhibitions to provide a high-quality and uniformed introduction to the artist next to their art work which sometimes can translate into a loss of potential followers/buyers

Solution

An AR introduction that can be activated by a scan of QR code which is uniformed across venues and brings along a wow factor that can potentially increase audience
