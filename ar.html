Step 1. Quantitative Research (Survey)

Participants: 75
Questions: How likely are you going to use AR in your work

Result: Small Business Owners/Marketers, Educators and Artists have expressed strong interests in using AR in their work

Step 2. Primary Research (User Interview)

Interview Questions
  • 1. How do you see yourself using AR?
    (Discover potential usage of AR)
  • 2. How do you get assets for AR?
    (Evaluate user frictions in getting assets)
  • 3. Is there a specific problem you can solve with AR?
    ((To see if there's an immediate need)
  • 4. How much do you think AR can help you with your work?
    (To see how valuable our service to be to the user)
