My Story

First of all, thank you for clicking in and being interested.

I am a Developer with coffee addiction and experience in design. I found my passion for code through the program called Contemporary Web Design where I learned about mostly web development and some game developement and I became obsessed with it ever since.

But my journey for coding did not start there. I had a degree in Mechanical Engineering and I started programming Arduino and Robotics there and later on went on to become a manufacturing engineer where I wrote programs to control industrial machines but eventually I found my passion of writing code for digital experience and decided to pursue in that direction. And my design background really helps me to bridge the gap between design and development

Outside of design and development. I am also a Robotics/Programming Instructor for high school students where I lead them to Vex Robotics Competition / Hackathon / Game Jams

Also a huge Basketball Fan. Skipped a lot of classes back in my engineering days just to go hoop
